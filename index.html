01

Our Story

From the moment our paths first crossed, something shifted in the universe. What began as a chance encounter bloomed into something I never knew I was searching for.

Through quiet mornings and endless conversations, through every laugh and every shared silence, I found myself home in the simplest moments with you.

02

The Moments

The way you tilt your head when you laugh. How you hum softly when you cook. The warmth of your hand in mine during cold evening walks. These are the fragments I carry with me everywhere.

Every memory we've painted together has shaped the person I am today, and I want nothing more than to spend a lifetime creating more.

03

Forever

I used to think forever was just a word. A pretty concept that existed only in stories and poetry. But then I met you, and suddenly forever felt like it might not be long enough.

I want to grow old beside you. I want to weather every storm and celebrate every sunrise, hand in hand, heart to heart, for all the days to come.

—

So I suppose there is only one thing left to ask...

Will you marry me?
